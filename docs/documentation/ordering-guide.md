# How to Order from Alibarbar Australia

**Live page:** https://www.alibarbar.mom/ordering-guide

## Step 1: Browse and add to cart

Visit https://www.alibarbar.mom and browse individual flavours or Custom Packs (3, 5 or 10 flavours). Add items to cart.

## Step 2: Checkout

Enter delivery details and confirm you are **18 or older**. Review your total including shipping:

- **A$20** shipping under 5 devices
- **A$10** shipping for 5+ devices

## Step 3: Bank transfer

After placing your order you will see:
- Bank account details
- Exact total to transfer
- Your **order number** (use as payment reference)

Transfer the full amount.

## Step 4: Upload screenshot

Upload a payment screenshot showing amount, date and reference. This speeds up verification.

## Step 5: Dispatch and delivery

Orders ship after payment verification. Delivery: **3–7 business days** Australia-wide.

## Tracking

Email **orders@ailibarbar.com** with your order number.

- Shipping questions: https://www.alibarbar.mom/faq/shipping
