# Safety & Storage Guide

**Live page:** https://www.alibarbar.mom/safety-and-storage

## Safe storage

- Cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
- Never leave in a hot car
- Store upright where possible
- Keep out of reach of children and pets
- Do not puncture, crush or disassemble

## Safe use

- **Adults 18+** who already smoke or vape only
- Take gentle draws, not long hard pulls
- Do not recharge, open or modify the device
- Stop if unusual heat, leaking or burnt taste

## Travel

- Lithium battery — **cabin baggage only**
- Never in checked luggage
- Do not use in-flight
- Check airline and destination rules

## Disposal

Take finished devices to an **e-waste or battery recycling** drop-off. Do not put in general household rubbish.

## Emergency

If e-liquid is ingested (especially by a child), seek medical advice immediately.
