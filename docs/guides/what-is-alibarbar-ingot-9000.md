# What Is the Alibarbar Ingot 9000?

The **Alibarbar Ingot 9000** is a premium disposable vape built for adult users who want a long-lasting, no-fuss device. It arrives pre-filled, pre-charged and ready to use — no filling, charging or buttons.

## Key specifications

| Spec | Detail |
|------|--------|
| Puff count | Up to 9000 puffs |
| E-liquid | 22ml pre-filled |
| Battery | 2350mAh (non-rechargeable) |
| Display | Smart LED — battery & e-liquid levels |
| Activation | Inhale-activated |
| Weight | ~90g |

## Signature features

- **Gold ingot-bar design** — distinctive premium look
- **Smart LED display** — see remaining battery and e-liquid at a glance
- **Large capacity** — 22ml tank backs the 9000 puff rating
- **Matched battery** — 2350mAh sized to last the full e-liquid life

## Who it is for

Existing adult smokers and vapers in Australia who want a dependable device without refillable kit maintenance. **Not** for non-smokers, anyone under 18, or people who are pregnant or breastfeeding.
