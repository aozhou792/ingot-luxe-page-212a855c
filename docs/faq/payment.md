# Payment FAQ

**Live page:** https://www.alibarbar.mom/faq/payment

## What payment methods do you accept?

We accept **bank transfer** only. After placing your order you will see bank account details and the order total.

## How do I pay by bank transfer?

1. Place your order
2. Copy the bank details on the confirmation page
3. Transfer the **exact order total**
4. Use your **order number** as the payment reference
5. Upload your payment screenshot

## Why upload a payment screenshot?

The screenshot helps us match your transfer to your order faster. It should show the amount, date and reference.

## How long does verification take?

Verification is usually completed once your transfer is visible and matched. Bank processing times vary.

## What if I forgot my order number as reference?

Email orders@ailibarbar.com with your order number, transfer amount, time and a screenshot.

## Is checkout secure?

Yes. Checkout uses HTTPS. We do not store card numbers because payment is by bank transfer.

## What currency are prices in?

All prices are in **Australian dollars (AUD)**.
