## Is it rechargeable?

**No.** The Ingot 9000 is a non-rechargeable disposable. When the battery or e-liquid is finished, replace the whole device.

## How long does one device last?

At ~300 puffs/day, expect roughly **3–4 weeks**. Light users may get 6+ weeks; heavy users around 2 weeks.

## How to use

Remove from packaging, take off any cap, and inhale gently. No buttons, filling or charging.

## How to spot a fake

- Working LED display showing battery and e-liquid
- ALIBARBAR branding on packaging
- Consistent vapour and flavour quality
- Buy from a verified retailer

**Guide:** https://www.ailibarbar.com/guides/how-to-spot-fake-alibarbar-ingot

## Storage

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and extreme heat. Do not leave in a hot car.
