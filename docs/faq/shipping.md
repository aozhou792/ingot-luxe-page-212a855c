# Shipping FAQ

**Live page:** https://www.ailibarbar.com/faq/shipping

## How much is shipping?

Shipping is **A$20** for orders under 5 devices and **A$10** for orders of 5 or more devices. The fee is added automatically at checkout.

## Do you ship Australia-wide?

Yes. We ship to NSW, VIC, QLD, WA, SA, TAS, ACT and NT. Regional and remote areas may take longer than metro areas.

## How long does delivery take?

After your bank transfer is confirmed, delivery usually takes **3–7 business days** depending on your state and postcode.

## When will my order be dispatched?

Orders are dispatched after payment is verified. Use your order number as the bank transfer reference and upload your payment screenshot after ordering.

## Can I get tracking?

Email **orders@ailibarbar.com** with your order number and we can provide the latest delivery status.

## Do you ship to Sydney, Melbourne and Brisbane?

Yes. Metro deliveries are usually on the faster end of the 3–7 business day window.

## Do you ship internationally?

No. Alibarbar Australia focuses on Australian customers only.

## What if my item arrives damaged?

Email orders@ailibarbar.com within 7 days with your order number and photos of the issue.
