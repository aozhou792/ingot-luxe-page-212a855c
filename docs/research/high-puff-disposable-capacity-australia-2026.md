Original research note comparing Alibarbar Ingot 9000 capacity specs against typical Australian high-puff disposable ranges.

## Quick answer

In our 2026 desktop snapshot, the Alibarbar Ingot 9000 (up to 9000 puffs, 22ml, 2350mAh, LED display) sits at the high end of publicly listed high-puff disposable ranges commonly marketed to adult Australians.

## Methodology

- Manufacturer / inventory specs for Ingot 9000
- Public competitor ranges from editorial comparison pages (IGET, HQD, Gunnpod, KUZ)
- Fields: puff count, tank ml, battery mAh, display
- Reddit / YouTube used as qualitative context only
