Alibarbar packaging authenticity QR codes
==========================================

RIGHT QR (box entry — same on every box):
  entry-verify.png
  → https://www.alibarbar.mom/verify

LEFT HONEYCOMB (anti-counterfeit — pick any of these 5 to print):
  honeycomb-ABSEAL01.png → https://www.alibarbar.mom/verify?seal=ABSEAL01
  honeycomb-ABSEAL02.png → https://www.alibarbar.mom/verify?seal=ABSEAL02
  honeycomb-ABSEAL03.png → https://www.alibarbar.mom/verify?seal=ABSEAL03
  honeycomb-ABSEAL04.png → https://www.alibarbar.mom/verify?seal=ABSEAL04
  honeycomb-ABSEAL05.png → https://www.alibarbar.mom/verify?seal=ABSEAL05

Customer flow:
  1. Scan right QR → opens /verify
  2. On that page, scan or upload the left honeycomb QR
  3. Matching seal → Genuine page; otherwise → Not genuine page
